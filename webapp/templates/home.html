Welcome to EvoDraughts

This is my Final Year Project where I'm using Grammatical Evolution (GE) with the GRAPE framework to evolve draughts playing strategies. I've trained AI opponents through genetic algorithms, and you can test them out here. The strategies improve over generations by playing games and learning what works.

What You Can Do

Multiple Difficulty Levels

I've organized the evolved strategies into Easy, Medium, and Hard categories based on how well they perform. Each represents different stages of the evolution process.

Board Size Options

You can choose between a simplified 6x6 board for quicker games, or the full 8x8 standard draughts board for the traditional experience.

Evolved Strategies

All the AI opponents you'll face are strategies that evolved through my grammatical evolution system, not pre-programmed rules.

Interactive Gameplay

Play real games with visual feedback. The AI will make its moves automatically after yours, and you can see exactly what's happening.

Ready to Play?

Pick your board size and difficulty, then give it a go. Right now games are not being recorded!

About This Project

For my Final Year Project, I wanted to explore how Grammatical Evolution could be applied to game AI. The strategies are written as expressions using a Backus-Naur Form (BNF) grammar, and then evolved over generations using genetic algorithms. Each generation plays games, and the better strategies are selected to create the next generation.
